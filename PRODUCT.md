# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Fellow hiking enthusiasts — people who love being in the mountains and are looking for route inspiration, trail stories, and a personal perspective on places they've been or want to go. They read for the experience of it, not to get specs. They want to feel the trail through someone else's words.

## Product Purpose

A personal mountain-hiking hobby website sharing favourite routes and trail stories. The goal is to inspire, invite, and take the reader along — not to inform abstractly, but to put them on the trail beside you. Success is a visitor who finishes a page and wants to go outside.

## Brand Personality

Personal, Warm, Adventurous. The voice of a trusted trail friend, not a brand or publication. Direct and honest. Enthusiastic without performing enthusiasm. The reader should feel like they got a personal recommendation, not a press release.

## Anti-references

- Corporate outdoors brands (REI, North Face campaign sites): product-marketing energy, glossy, impersonal
- Minimalist-cool: all white, cold space, clinical type — feels distant and designed-to-impress
- Outdoorsy-rugged cliché: dark olive, distressed textures, wood grain, faux-vintage — tries too hard to "be outdoors"
- Travel-blog generic: stock-photo heroes, "Top 10" bullet lists, nested cards

## Design Principles

1. **The route is the story.** Content leads, design follows. Let the writing breathe. No decorative noise that competes with the words.
2. **Take them along.** Every design choice should feel like an invitation, not a presentation. Warmth over polish.
3. **Earned confidence, not performance.** Personal and direct. The site earns trust through voice, not visual spectacle.
4. **Sensory over decorative.** Details that evoke the trail — light, scale, texture — over details that just look nice.
5. **One voice, every page.** No tonal or visual shifts between pages. The guide is always the same person.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as baseline. No specific user needs stated. Standard reduced-motion support for any animations.
