My most beautiful route

Yellowstone National Park — Fairy Falls & Grand Prismatic Overlook

There is one route I keep coming back to. One that never loses its magic. This is it.

We start at the trailhead just off Fountain Flat Drive. The path is flat at first — easy, deceptive. Pine trees line both sides. The air smells of sulphur and earth. Already, Yellowstone is reminding you this is no ordinary place.

Then we climb. Just a short, sharp ascent to the Grand Prismatic overlook. And that is when it happens. The full colour of the spring unfolds below you. Electric blue at the centre. Rings of green, yellow, and deep orange spreading outward like a sunrise. Nothing I have ever seen compares to it.

We push on to Fairy Falls — 60 metres of cold water tumbling into a quiet pool. Sit here for a moment. Feel the mist on your face. You have earned it.

The return leg is pure contentment. Legs tired, mind clear, heart full. That is what this route does to you every single time.

Trust me. Do this one.
