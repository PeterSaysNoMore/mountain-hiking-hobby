About this site

I am not a professional mountaineer. I am not a guide. I am someone who found the mountains and never quite managed to leave them behind.

This site is a record of that. The routes I have walked. The places that stopped me in my tracks. The mornings where the mist sat low in the valley and the world felt very large and very quiet all at once.

I built this to share what I know — which is not everything, but it is real. Every route here I have walked myself. Every word here I mean. If one of these trails finds its way onto your list, that is more than enough.

Lace up. The mountain is waiting.
